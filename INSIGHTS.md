# Gameplay Insights - LILA BLACK

Based on the analysis of the February 10-14 dataset using the Visualization Tool.

## 1. Bot-Centric Combat Loop
**Observation**: In the sampled matches, over 95% of combat events were `BotKill` (Human killing a Bot).
- **Evidence**: 32 BotKills vs. 1 Human-on-Human Kill in the analyzed sample.
- **Recommendation**: Consider increasing bot difficulty or reducing bot density in high-tier loot zones to encourage more meaningful PvP (Player vs. Player) interactions.

## 2. Storm Positioning & Lethality
**Observation**: Environmental deaths (`KilledByStorm`) are extremely rare.
- **Evidence**: Only 1 recorded storm death across the analyzed matches.
- **Recommendation**: Level designers could analyze the "Player Density" heatmap during the final match phases. If players are comfortably staying ahead of the storm without conflict, consider increasing the storm's speed or damage in the final circles.

## 3. Map Utilization & Looting Patterns
**Observation**: Looting events are evenly distributed across maps, but "Ambrose Valley" shows higher combat density in the central regions.
- **Evidence**: Ambrose Valley had 59 loot events vs. 46 in Grand Rift.
- **Recommendation**: Check the "Kill Density" heatmap for Lockdown. If combat is concentrated in only one building, consider redistributing loot to the outskirts to utilize the full map area.
